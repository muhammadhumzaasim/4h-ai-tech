Our Story

At a young age of 9 years, I dreamt of creating my own games, but I quickly realized I lacked the necessary skills. Undeterred, I embarked on a journey of self-improvement and began honing my game development skills through practice. During my early school years, an opportunity arose when my teacher introduced me to coding using MS Small Basic. It was my first formal exposure to programming, and I eagerly absorbed the knowledge. This newfound skill set allowed me to take my first steps in coding, merging my two passions. As I grew older, I temporarily set aside game development to focus on playing games more extensively. However, my interest in coding never waned. With time, I became proficient in Python, and at the age of 13, I embarked on creating captivating projects and software programs. Although I was not yet a master, I had a solid foundation. Today, my learning journey continues, and I constantly strive to enhance my skills. I channel my creativity and knowledge into various projects, which I proudly showcase on my website.
